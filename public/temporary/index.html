About Us

We Are In A Mission To Feed INDIA

Over 20 CRORE Indians go to sleep empty-stomach every day and around 172,000 TONS of food gets wasted everyday.

The gap between surplus food from the food industry and those in need remains unaddressed, resulting in a waste of valuable resources and a failure to support local communities.

Sustainable Development Goals

Making a Difference, Together.

Good Health and Well-Being

We provides access to nutritious meals to those who may otherwise go hungry. By providing access to nutritious food, it will improve health outcomes.

Zero Hunger

We help to reduce food waste and provide nutritious meals to those in need, working towards achieving zero hunger.

Partnership for the Goals

We bring together Food-Suppliers and Non-profit Organizations to work towards a common goal, promoting partnerships and collaboration towards the SDGs.
